Given n full water bottles, you can exchange m empty water bottles for one full water bottle.
The operation of drinking a full water bottle turns it into an empty bottle.
Return the maximum number of water bottles you can drink.
For example, n=9 and m=3

Input Description:
n and m as integer. (1<n,m<1000)

Output Description:
Print the maximum number of water bottles you can drink.

Sample Input:
9 3

Sample Output:
13

Explanation:
Consider the given explanation in the question.

Sample Output:
24

Sample Output:
53

100
